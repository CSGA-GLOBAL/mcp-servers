Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to frequently asked questions about getting started with CSGA.

How do I become a CSGA member?
Visit our Membership page to explore our tiers. Community membership is free and gives you access to basic resources, newsletters, and community forums. Professional and Enterprise tiers offer additional benefits including certification access and priority support.
What is the CASA-CA30 certification?
CASA-CA30 (Constitutional AI Security Auditor) is our professional certification for AI governance specialists. It covers AI risk assessment, constitutional AI principles, CSOAI framework implementation, and MCP server governance. Visit our Education page for details.
Do you offer enterprise training programmes?
Yes. We provide customised training programmes for organisations including on-site workshops, virtual cohort training, and executive briefings. Enterprise packages include dedicated instructors, custom curriculum aligned to your industry, and bulk certification pricing. Contact our sales team for details.
How can my organisation partner with CSGA?
We offer several partnership models: technology partnerships, consulting partnerships, academic partnerships, and strategic alliances. Partners gain access to co-marketing opportunities, joint research programmes, and early access to standards development. Email partnerships@csgaglobal.com to start a conversation.
Are there discounts for veterans or students?
Absolutely. Veterans receive up to 50% off Professional membership and certification fees through our Veterans Support Programme. Full-time students and educators qualify for academic pricing. Contact our membership team for eligibility details.
What security clearances are needed for Defence programmes?
Requirements vary by programme. Our Defence AI Forum and classified training tracks require appropriate national security clearances. For Five Eyes collaborative programmes, clearances must be recognised by participating nations. Contact our defence team at defence@csgaglobal.com for specific requirements.
