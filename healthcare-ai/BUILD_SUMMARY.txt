CORE COMPONENTS:
================================================================================

1. TOOLS (4 Assessment Tools)
   
   Tool 1: hipaa_ai_assessment
   - Assesses HIPAA Privacy/Security compliance for AI systems
   - Evaluates: PHI risk level, applicable rules, required safeguards
   - Returns: Risk classification, gaps, remediation steps, timeline
   - Key Focus: Protected Health Information (PHI) protection

   Tool 2: fda_ai_assessment
   - Determines FDA regulatory pathway for AI/ML medical devices
   - Evaluates: SaMD classification, device class, PCCP requirements
   - Returns: Regulatory pathway, quality system requirements, timeline
   - Key Focus: FDA AI/ML-Based SaMD Action Plan compliance

   Tool 3: clinical_ai_safety
   - Comprehensive clinical safety assessment with bias analysis
   - Evaluates: Risk per EU AI Act, safety concerns, bias risks
   - Returns: Validation requirements, CASA tier, human oversight model
   - Key Focus: Clinical decision support safety & fairness

   Tool 4: eu_mdr_compliance
   - EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR 2017/745) assessment
   - Evaluates: MDR classification, conformity route, notified body need
   - Returns: Technical documentation requirements, post-market surveillance
   - Key Focus: CE marking & EU regulatory compliance

2. RESOURCES (2 Reference Resources)

   Resource 1: healthcare://regulations/index
   - Complete regulatory landscape reference
   - Covers: HIPAA, FDA, EU MDR, EU AI Act, international standards

   Resource 2: healthcare://tools/guide
   - Comprehensive tool documentation
   - Includes: Usage examples, workflows, best practices

USAGE EXAMPLES:
================================================================================

EXAMPLE 1: Diagnostic AI System
---
Tool: hipaa_ai_assessment
Inputs:
  system_name: "Diagnostic AI System"
  system_description: "ML model for cancer diagnosis using medical imaging"
  phi_data_types: "diagnostic images, patient demographics, treatment history"
  deployment_environment: "cloud"
Output:
  - HIPAA category: Covered Entity — Healthcare Provider
  - PHI risk level: HIGH
  - Required safeguards for cloud deployment
  - Gap analysis: 4-5 gaps identified
  - Timeline: 9-12 months

EXAMPLE 2: FDA Regulatory Pathway
---
Tool: fda_ai_assessment
Inputs:
  system_name: "Cancer Detection SaMD"
  intended_use: "assist radiologists in detecting lung cancer from CT scans"
  clinical_context: "diagnosis and treatment decision support"
  ai_model_type: "deep learning convolutional neural network"
Output:
  - SaMD Category: III
  - Device Classification: Class II (Special Controls)
  - Regulatory Pathway: De Novo Classification
  - PCCP Required: No (static model)
  - Timeline: 12-18 months

EXAMPLE 3: Clinical Safety Assessment
---
Tool: clinical_ai_safety
Inputs:
  system_name: "Diagnostic AI"
  clinical_domain: "radiology and pathology"
  patient_population: "adult and geriatric patients"
  decision_autonomy: "decision support — clinician makes final determination"
Output:
  - Risk Classification: High Risk (EU AI Act)
  - Safety Concerns: 7 identified (bias, drift, alert fatigue, etc.)
  - Bias Assessment: Demographic risks + mitigation strategies
  - CASA Tier: Tier 3 (Enterprise Certification)
  - Validation Requirements: Prospective clinical study required
  - Human Oversight: Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) model

EXAMPLE 4: EU Market Compliance
---
Tool: eu_mdr_compliance
Inputs:
  system_name: "Diagnostic Device"
  device_description: "AI software analyzing medical images using deep learning"
  intended_purpose: "diagnosis of cancer from imaging"
  risk_class: "Class IIb"
Output:
  - MDR Classification: Class IIb
  - Conformity Route: Annex IX (QMS) + Clinical Evaluation
  - Notified Body Required: Yes
  - Essential Requirements: 9 listed (GSPR, software lifecycle, risk mgmt, etc.)
  - Timeline: 18-24 months
  - Post-market obligations: PMS Plan, vigilance reporting, etc.
