What is TSO-CSRT?

The Threat Security Operations - Cyber Security Response Team (TSO-CSRT) is CSGA's elite emergency response unit dedicated to rapid incident response, threat mitigation, and recovery support for critical infrastructure and defence-sector organizations facing cyber attacks, data breaches, and security emergencies.

How TSO-CSRT Operates

Rapid Response: On-call incident response teams available immediately upon notification. Average response time: 15 minutes for critical incidents.

Deep Expertise: Forensic specialists, malware analysts, threat hunters, and defence security experts with government and military experience.

Confidentiality: All incident information classified and protected under security clearance protocols. Legal privilege and confidentiality maintained throughout engagement.

Continuity Support: Beyond incident response, we provide recovery planning, system restoration, and post-incident analysis.

Incident Response Process

1

Detection & Activation

Report confirmed or suspected incident. TSO-CSRT team activated immediately with incident commander assigned.

2

Containment

Isolate affected systems, preserve evidence, and prevent spread of compromise to minimize damage and impact.

3

Investigation & Analysis

Forensic analysis, threat identification, compromise assessment, and root cause determination.

4

Recovery & Hardening

System restoration, vulnerability remediation, and security hardening to prevent recurrence.
