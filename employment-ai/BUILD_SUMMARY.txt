COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================

FOR HIRING AI SYSTEMS:
  • Disparate impact analysis across ALL protected classes
  • Four-Fifths (80%) Rule testing per EEOC guidelines
  • Selection rate comparison by demographic at each hiring stage
  • Intersectional bias testing (race×gender, age×disability, etc.)
  • Alternative selection procedure analysis
  • Historical training data bias audit
  • Proxy variable detection
  • Annual third-party bias audit (NYC LL144 mandatory)
  • Candidate notification 10+ business days before AEDT use
  • Public posting of bias audit summary on company website
  • Candidate data rights portal (access, explanation, erasure)
  • Right to human review for all AI-screened candidates
  • Right to explanation of AI-based hiring decisions
  • Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment (FRIA) — EU AI Act
  • Register system with EU AI Act registry (if EU operations)

FOR WORKPLACE SURVEILLANCE SYSTEMS:
  • PROHIBITION of emotion recognition (EU AI Act Article 5(1)(f))
  • Employee notification about monitoring and purpose
  • Proportionality assessment (6-point framework):
    1. Legitimate business purpose
    2. Necessity (least intrusive means)
    3. Proportionality (benefits vs. privacy)
    4. Data minimization (only necessary data)
    5. Time limitation (work hours only)
    6. Transparency (employees fully informed)
  • Employee data access portal
  • Employee contestation/appeal process
  • Union/employee representative involvement
  • Annual workplace surveillance impact assessment
  • Right to disconnect (no after-hours monitoring)
  • Right to human review of monitoring-based decisions
