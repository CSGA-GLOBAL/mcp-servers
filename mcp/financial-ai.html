Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Financial AI Governance.

What is Financial AI Governance?
Financial AI Governance is an enterprise MCP tool designed to validate and monitor AI systems across banking, insurance, and capital markets. It ensures compliance with SOX, Basel III/IV, MiFID II, and Dodd-Frank requirements with automated policy enforcement and real-time audit trails.
Which financial regulations does this support?
Full support for SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley), Basel III and IV, MiFID II, Dodd-Frank Act, and common industry standards. Extensible to include additional regulatory frameworks.
How quickly can I integrate Financial AI Governance?
Most teams are up and running within 15 minutes using our Python SDK or REST API. Our quick start guide provides copy-paste code examples. Enterprise customers receive white-glove onboarding support.
Can I audit existing trading algorithms?
Yes. The Algorithmic Trading Auditor tool can validate existing models for fairness bias, market manipulation risk, and regulatory compliance. It supports real-time and historical backtesting scenarios.
