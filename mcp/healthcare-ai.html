Frequently Asked Questions

Is this MCP HIPAA compliant?
‚ñº
Yes. The Healthcare AI Governance MCP is built with HIPAA compliance as a foundational principle. All tools validate against HIPAA Privacy Rule, Security Rule, and Breach Notification requirements. We offer Business Associate Agreement (BAA) agreements for enterprise customers.
What healthcare regulations does this cover?
‚ñº
The MCP covers HIPAA (US), FDA AI/ML guidance, EMA AI Act requirements, and state privacy laws (CCPA, GDPR for EU). Clinical risk assessment aligns with FDA premarket and post-market requirements for AI/ML-based software as a medical device (SaMD).
Can I integrate this with my existing AI/ML infrastructure?
‚ñº
Yes. The Healthcare AI Governance MCP is designed to work with any AI system. It integrates via standard APIs and provides compliance checks without requiring modifications to your existing models or infrastructure.
How does the bias detection work for clinical models?
‚ñº
The clinical_ai_risk_assessment tool performs fairness analysis across protected demographic characteristics (age, gender, race, etc.), calculates disparate impact ratios, and identifies underperforming subgroups. Reports highlight bias and recommend mitigation strategies.
What patient data types are supported for classification?
‚ñº
The patient_data_classifier supports all PHI categories: identifiers, medical records, diagnoses, medications, lab results, imaging data, genetic information, and behavioral health data. It provides HIPAA Safe Harbor de-identification recommendations.
Is there support for continuous compliance monitoring?
‚ñº
Yes. Professional and Enterprise tiers include continuous monitoring, automated audit trail generation, alert systems for compliance deviations, and regular compliance reporting for regulatory documentation.
