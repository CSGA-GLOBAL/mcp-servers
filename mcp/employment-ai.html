The Challenge

Employment AI systems face growing regulatory requirements and bias risks

Compliance Opacity

Hiring AI decisions lack transparency and audit trails for regulatory compliance with NYC LL144 and EEOC guidelines.

Demographic Bias

Undetected demographic bias in recruiting systems violates EEOC AI hiring guidelines and exposes organizations to discrimination claims.

Manual Auditing

Manual compliance reviews consume hundreds of hours per quarter and require specialized legal expertise.

Pay Equity Risk

Pay equity analysis requires expensive consultants and lacks real-time monitoring across hiring systems.

Example API Usage

import { EmploymentAI } from '@csoai/employment-ai';

const ai = new EmploymentAI({
  apiKey: 'your-api-key',
  organization: 'acme-corp'
});

// Audit hiring AI decision
const audit = await ai.hiring_ai_auditor.audit({
  candidateId: 'cand-12345',
  decisionType: 'offer',
  framework: 'nyc-ll144'
});

// Detect bias
const bias = await ai.workforce_bias_detector.analyze({
  timeRange: '90d',
  groupBy: ['gender', 'race', 'age']
});

// Check pay equity
const equity = await ai.compensation_ai_validator.assess({
  positions: ['Software Engineer', 'Product Manager'],
  includeVariance: true
});

What is Employment AI Governance and why do I need it? ‚ñº
Employment AI Governance provides automated compliance monitoring and bias detection for hiring AI systems. It helps organizations meet NYC LL144 and EEOC requirements while reducing legal and reputational risk from biased hiring decisions.

Yes, we support integrations with Workday, SAP SuccessFactors, Greenhouse, Lever, iCIMS, SmartRecruiters, ADP, and BambooHR. We also accept CSV/JSON uploads for non-integrated systems.
