Frequently Asked Questions

Does Insurance AI Governance support legacy underwriting systems? ‚Ä∫
Yes. We support API integration with legacy COBOL, Python, and proprietary underwriting systems. Our SDKs provide model-agnostic fairness auditing via decision tree analysis, feature importance, and statistical parity testing.
What makes Insurance AI different from generic AI governance? ‚Ä∫
We specialize in insurance-specific risks: protected class discrimination in underwriting (age, gender, health), speed equity in claims (claims time vs. fraud detection fairness), and actuarial model compliance with Solvency II and NAIC guidelines. Generic governance tools lack insurance regulatory context.
How does Solvency II compliance automation work? ‚Ä∫
We validate AI models against Article 22 (Governance) and Article 49 (Own Risk and Solvency Assessment) requirements. Our platform auto-generates risk registers, fairness reports, and documentation for insurance regulator submissions.
What happens if we detect discrimination in our models? ‚Ä∫
We generate detailed bias reports with remediation steps: removing proxy variables, retraining with fairness constraints, or adjusting pricing models. All changes are logged for regulatory audit trails. You maintain full control of remediation decisions.
Can we use Insurance AI Governance for reinsurance underwriting? ‚Ä∫
Yes. Reinsurers audit downstream cedent underwriting models for regulatory consistency and fairness. We provide aggregated compliance reporting across all partner models and risk classes.
Is Insurance AI Governance GDPR and data privacy compliant? ‚Ä∫
Yes. We process model decisions and fairness metrics only‚Äînever raw policyholder data. All audits are conducted in your secure environment or our GDPR-compliant EU cloud. We never store or export personal data.
