Frequently Asked Questions

What is a digital twin and how does it differ from a synthetic persona? ‚Ä∫
A digital twin is a virtual replica tied to a specific real individual for modeling and simulation. A synthetic persona is an AI-generated character not necessarily tied to a real person. Our governance applies to both with consent requirements.
How do we ensure consent for digital twin creation? ‚Ä∫
Our Digital Twin Governor verifies explicit informed consent before creation or use, with granular control over specific applications, time limits, and withdrawal mechanisms.
What happens to digital identities after death? ‚Ä∫
Our Legacy Management tools allow individuals to designate executors, configure posthumous access rules, and establish memorialization protocols respecting family wishes.
Can we detect unauthorized synthetic replicas? ‚Ä∫
Yes. Our Synthetic Persona Auditor uses behavioral biometrics, voice/face analysis, and pattern matching to identify unauthorized digital doubles with high confidence.
