About the Institute

The CSGA AI Research Institute is a leading center for AI security and governance research, advancing the science of secure human-AI systems and enterprise AI deployment. The institute brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders to solve critical challenges of AI safety, governance, and ethical deployment.

Our Mission: To advance AI security research while establishing internationally recognized standards for secure, ethical, and responsible AI development and deployment.
