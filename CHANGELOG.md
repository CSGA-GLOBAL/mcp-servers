- **Revenue optimisation** — Social proof, urgency indicators, testimonials, trust badges, cross-sell panels
- **GitHub community files** — README, CONTRIBUTING, CODE_OF_CONDUCT, SECURITY, LICENSE (MIT), issue templates, PR template

- **Standards alignment** — ISO 27001, NIST CSF, EU AI Act, SOC 2, GDPR, MITRE ATT&CK
