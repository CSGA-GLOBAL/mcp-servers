Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Community tier really free?
Yes. The Community tier gives you full access to all 36 MOOC courses, community forums, monthly newsletters, and webinar archives at no cost. No credit card is required to sign up.
What is CASA-CA30 certification?
CASA-CA30 is the Constitutional AI Safety Assurance certification — a 4-tier professional credential covering AI governance, security, and compliance. It is recognised across allied governments and industry partners.
Can I switch between tiers?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.
What MCP servers are included?
Professional members get access to 5 core MCP servers for compliance checking, threat analysis, and governance automation. Enterprise members unlock all 13 servers including sector-specific and defence-grade tools.
Do you offer discounts for veterans or students?
Yes. We offer dedicated programmes for veterans transitioning to AI security careers and discounted rates for academic researchers and students. Visit our Veterans or Education pages for details.
How does enterprise billing work?
Enterprise plans are customised to your organisation. We offer annual invoicing, purchase order support, and flexible seat licensing. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements.
