Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time through the Stripe billing portal. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated charges. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and wire transfers for Enterprise plans. Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount across all tiers.

Is there a free trial?

All paid memberships (Starter through Enterprise Full) include a 30-day free trial. No credit card required to start the Community tier. Individual MCPs come with a 14-day trial.

What's included in support?

Community: Forums only. Starter: Email (48h response). Professional: Priority email (24h). Enterprise Sector: Dedicated CSM with 8h SLA. Enterprise Full: Dedicated CSM with 4h SLA and quarterly on-site. Enterprise Custom: 24/7 on-site support with security-cleared teams.

Can I buy individual MCPs instead of a membership?

Yes! Any MCP can be purchased individually. Low-Value Products start at $9/month, Medium-Value at $29/month, and High-Value at $79/month. Each includes its own credit allocation. Upgrade to a membership anytime for better value.

What are ecosystem bundles?

Ecosystem bundles are curated groups of MCPs for specific verticals ‚Äî Security ($149/mo), Governance ($99/mo), DevOps ($79/mo), Defence ($199/mo), Industry ($129/mo), and Data ($39/mo). They cost less than buying individual MCPs separately and include higher credit allocations.

What is CASA certification?

The Cognitive AI Safety Assessment (CASA) is an industry-recognised certification for AI governance maturity. Four levels (CA-10 through CA-40) provide increasing rigour ‚Äî from self-assessment ($2,500) to sovereign-grade audits with FedRAMP alignment ($25,000).

What happens if I exceed my API credits?

Overage billing activates automatically at your tier's overage rate ($0.20-$0.08 per additional credit depending on plan). You can also purchase one-time credit packs starting at $9 for 1,000 credits. Enterprise Custom plans include unlimited API calls.

Do you offer custom deployment options?

Yes. Enterprise Sector and Full plans support hybrid deployment. Enterprise Custom includes air-gapped, sovereign, and FedRAMP-aligned deployment options. Contact our sales team for architecture review.
